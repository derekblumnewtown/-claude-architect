# POSITION AVAILABLE: Senior Materials Science Researcher

## Company
Helix Biotech Solutions - Cambridge, MA Area

## About Us
Helix Biotech Solutions is a mid-stage biotech firm focused on developing next-generation polymeric materials for medical device applications. We've been operating since 2015 and have grown to 87 employees across our research, manufacturing, and commercial divisions.

## The Role
We're seeking a Senior Materials Science Researcher to join our R&D team on a full-time basis. You'll lead the characterization and development of novel biodegradable polymers for surgical implants. This position reports directly to our VP of Research and involves both bench work and cross-functional collaboration with our engineering and regulatory teams.

Your primary responsibilities will include:
- Designing and executing materials testing protocols
- Mentoring 2-3 junior researchers
- Preparing technical documentation for FDA submissions
- Presenting findings at industry conferences
- Managing a budget of approximately $180K annually for lab supplies and equipment

## Required Qualifications
PhD in Materials Science, Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, or related field. Minimum 8 years post-doctoral experience in polymer science or materials characterization. Must have hands-on experience with: thermal analysis (DSC, TGA), microscopy techniques (SEM, optical), mechanical testing, and polymer synthesis. Experience with FDA regulatory submissions preferred but not required.

## Compensation & Benefits
Salary range: $125,000 - $165,000 annually based on experience. Comprehensive benefits package including health insurance (medical, dental, vision), 401(k) matching up to 4%, 25 days PTO, and professional development budget of $3,000/year.

## Location
Cambridge, MA (on-site 4 days/week; 1 day remote negotiable)
