SENIOR SPATIAL DATA ANALYST
Geospatial Solutions Inc. | Remote (US-based)

We're looking for a Senior Spatial Data Analyst to join our growing team at Geospatial Solutions Inc. This is a unique role that sits at the intersection of environmental science, urban planning, and software development.

Your primary responsibilities will include developing spatial analysis workflows for municipal clients, managing large geospatial datasets (think multi-terabyte LiDAR point clouds and satellite imagery archives), and mentoring junior analysts. You'll work with open-source GIS tools like QGIS and PostGIS alongside proprietary platforms. Some days you'll be writing Python scripts to automate data pipelines; other days you'll present findings to city planners or environmental agencies.

What we're looking for:
- 5+ years working with geospatial data (GIS, remote sensing, or similar)
- Strong Python or R skills for spatial analysis and data manipulation
- Experience with at least one major GIS platform (ArcGIS, QGIS, or open-source alternatives)
- Familiarity with spatial databases, ideally PostGIS
- Ability to communicate complex findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Bachelor's degree in geography, geomatics, environmental science, computer science, or related field (or equivalent professional experience)

Nice to have: Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP), machine learning applications in geospatial work, experience with web mapping libraries (Leaflet, Mapbox).

This is a fully remote position reporting to our Director of Analysis. We offer flexible hours, professional development support, and a collaborative environment where your insights directly influence client outcomes.

Interested? Send your resume, a brief cover letter, and a portfolio link (GitHub, personal projects, or published work) to careers@geospatialsolutions.local.
