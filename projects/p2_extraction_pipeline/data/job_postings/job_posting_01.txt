**POSITION AVAILABLE: Production Scheduling Coordinator**

Beachtree Manufacturing LLC is seeking a detail-oriented Production Scheduling Coordinator to join our Operations team. We are a mid-sized custom fabrication shop specializing in architectural metalwork and have been serving commercial clients throughout the Pacific Northwest for 18 years.

The ideal candidate will manage our production queue, coordinate with shop floor supervisors, and communicate delivery timelines to clients. You'll use our ERP system (currently NetSuite, transitioning to a custom platform) to track job progress from intake through shipping. This role requires someone who can juggle competing priorities, think logically about resource constraints, and remain calm during inevitable schedule disruptions.

**What You'll Do:**
- Receive job specifications from sales and create detailed production schedules
- Coordinate with fabrication, welding, and finishing departments daily
- Update clients on project status and manage expectation-setting
- Maintain material inventory visibility and flag long-lead-item concerns
- Troubleshoot scheduling conflicts when equipment breaks down or staff calls out
- Produce weekly production reports for management review

**What We're Looking For:**
You have experience in a manufacturing or job-shop environment. Proficiency with ERP systems is valuable but trainable if you demonstrate strong spreadsheet skills and logical thinking. We need someone with at least 2–3 years in a similar coordination or planning role, though an exceptionally organized operations assistant could be considered. Ability to communicate clearly with both office staff and hands-on shop workers is essential. A background in metalworking or fabrication is a nice-to-have but not required.

**Logistics:**
Based in Tacoma, WA (no remote work). 40 hours/week, Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM start. $48,000–$55,000 depending on experience.

Please send resume and a brief note about your manufacturing experience to careers@beachtreemfg.com. We review applications on a rolling basis.
