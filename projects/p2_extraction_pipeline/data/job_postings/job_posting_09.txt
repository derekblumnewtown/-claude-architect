# Senior Content Strategist – Healthcare Technology

**Company:** Meridian Digital Health Solutions

**Location:** Remote (US-based, candidates must be within EST/CST time zones)

**Employment Type:** Full-Time, Exempt

---

## About Us

Meridian Digital Health Solutions develops cloud-based patient engagement platforms for regional hospital networks and independent practices. We're a Series B startup with 85 employees across multiple states, and we're looking for someone who can elevate how we communicate with both clinical and administrative audiences.

## The Role

You'll own the content strategy across our product platform, customer-facing website, educational resources, and emerging channels. This isn't a writing role—it's about defining *what* we say, *to whom*, and *when*. You'll partner with product, marketing, design, and clinical teams to ensure consistency while adapting messaging for different buyer personas and use cases.

Your day-to-day involves content audits, competitive analysis, voice and tone documentation, user research interpretation, developing content calendars, and mentoring our junior content creator (hired recently). You'll also own documentation strategy for our compliance and regulatory needs, which is uniquely important in healthcare tech.

We expect you to understand healthcare workflows, regulatory language, and the gap between how providers actually work and how software vendors talk about them.

## Who You Are

- You've spent 4+ years in content strategy, preferably in B2B SaaS, healthcare, fintech, or regulated industries
- You're comfortable with ambiguity and can define structure where none exists
- You have demonstrable experience conducting stakeholder interviews and translating findings into content frameworks
- You're familiar with SEO basics and analytics (Google Analytics, at minimum)
- You've managed cross-functional projects and can diplomatically push back on requests
- You write clearly and quickly; you can produce everything from 1,000-word thought leadership pieces to microcopy
- You understand HIPAA concepts at a functional level (not necessarily compliance expertise, but enough to ask the right questions)

## What You Won't Do

We're not looking for someone to manage social media full-time, design graphics, or own the entire marketing funnel. You won't be a copywriter taking dictation from executives. This is strategy-first.

## What We Offer

- $92,000–$118,000 annually (based on background and experience)
- Health, dental, vision insurance (company covers 85% of premiums)
- 401(k) with 3% company match
- Unlimited PTO (genuine, tracked in aggregate only)
- $2,000 annual professional development budget
- Occasional travel for customer research and team offsites (2–3 times per year)

Send a resume, brief cover letter, and a single example of content strategy work (can be an anonymized case study, framework, audit, or portfolio piece) to hiring@meridiandigitalhealth.com. Include "Content Strategy – [Your Name]" in the subject line.
