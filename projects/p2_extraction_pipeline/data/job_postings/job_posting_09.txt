# SENIOR GROWTH OPERATIONS STRATEGIST

**Company:** Momentum Labs (Series B SaaS)
**Location:** Remote (US-based preferred, willing to sponsor visa)
**Job Type:** Full-Time, Hybrid (quarterly in-person retreats in Austin, TX)

## About Us

Momentum Labs builds workflow automation software for mid-market professional services firms. We've grown 240% YoY and just closed our Series B. Our team is distributed across 12 countries, but we maintain a strong culture of async-first collaboration with intentional synchronous moments.

## The Role

You'll own the intersection of revenue operations, data analytics, and process optimization. This isn't a traditional ops role—you'll be part strategy consultant, part data scientist, part process engineer. Reporting to our VP of Revenue, you'll work cross-functionally with Sales, Customer Success, Finance, and Product to identify growth bottlenecks and implement systems-level solutions.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Design and maintain dashboards that tell the story of our business (we use Looker, but open to other tools)
- Conduct deep-dive analyses of sales cycle, conversion rates, and expansion revenue patterns
- Build forecasting models and scenario planning frameworks
- Implement or optimize our CRM/billing system stack (currently Salesforce + Zuora)
- Run experiments and A/B tests on go-to-market approaches
- Create playbooks and documentation that scale our processes without adding headcount

## Requirements

**Must Have:**
- 5-7 years experience in revenue operations, business analytics, or growth strategy roles
- SQL proficiency (comfortable writing moderately complex queries)
- Experience with Salesforce OR similar CRM platforms
- Strong statistical thinking (you understand p-values, confidence intervals, and how to avoid common data traps)
- Excellent communication skills—you synthesize complex data into actionable insights for non-technical stakeholders

**Nice to Have:**
- Python or R for data analysis
- Exposure to SaaS metrics and unit economics
- Familiarity with tools like dbt, Amplitude, or Mixpanel
- Experience with subscription billing models
- Background in management consulting
