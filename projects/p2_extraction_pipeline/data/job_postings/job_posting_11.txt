# POSITION POSTING

**Title:** Aquaculture Systems Technician

**Organization:** Cascade Marine Innovations LLC

**Location:** Port Angeles, WA (maritime operations)

---

## Overview

We're seeking an experienced Aquaculture Systems Technician to maintain and optimize our recirculating aquaculture system (RAS) facility. This is a hands-on role combining mechanical troubleshooting, water chemistry management, and data logging for our sustainable fish farming operation.

## Key Responsibilities

- Monitor and calibrate dissolved oxygen, pH, ammonia, and nitrate levels across multiple tank systems daily
- Perform preventive maintenance on pumps, biofilters, and UV sterilization units
- Troubleshoot system failures and implement corrective actions with minimal downtime
- Maintain detailed records of water parameters and fish health observations
- Coordinate with hatchery and grow-out managers on stocking densities and feeding protocols
- Occasional on-call availability for emergency system failures (comp time provided)

## Required Qualifications

5+ years working with recirculating systems OR equivalent aquaculture facility experience. Proficiency with SCADA software preferred. Valid driver's license. Comfortable working in wet conditions and at heights.

## Nice to Have

- Welding certification
- Knowledge of commercial fish species biology
- Certification in aquatic facility management

## Compensation

$19.50–$23.75 hourly depending on experience. Health insurance after 90 days. 401(k) matching up to 3%.
