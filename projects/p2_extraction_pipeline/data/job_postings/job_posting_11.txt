COMMUNITY HEALTH EDUCATOR - AmeriCare Health Services

AmeriCare Health Services is seeking a Community Health Educator to join our Prevention & Wellness Division. This is a unique hybrid role that combines classroom instruction, one-on-one coaching, and community outreach programming.

Position Overview
You'll develop and deliver health education programs focused on chronic disease prevention, maternal health, and health literacy in underserved neighborhoods. Unlike traditional education roles, you'll split time between our main office (located in the Denver metro area), mobile clinics, and community partner sites throughout Colorado. There is no fixed office location — you'll work from home 2 days per week and travel to various community sites the remaining days.

Responsibilities
- Create culturally responsive educational materials and curricula for diverse populations
- Teach workshops on nutrition, diabetes management, and preventive care (8-12 participants per session)
- Conduct individual health coaching sessions via phone and in-person
- Partner with local nonprofits, churches, and schools to identify community needs
- Track program attendance and collect outcome data; prepare quarterly reports
- Occasionally present findings at state health conferences

Required Qualifications
Bachelor's degree in public health, nursing, health sciences, education, social work, or related field. Spanish fluency (verbal and written) is essential. Valid driver's license and reliable transportation. Strong comfort with diverse populations and ability to work independently.

Preferred: 2-3 years in health education, community health, or patient education. Certification as a health educator (CHES) or community health worker. Experience with Salesforce or program evaluation databases.

Compensation & Schedule
$42,000-$48,000 annually, depending on experience. 40 hours/week with flexible scheduling. Health insurance (medical, dental, vision), 15 PTO days, professional development stipend ($1,200/year).

To Apply
Submit resume, cover letter, and brief writing sample (2 pages max) to careers@americarehealth.org. Position available immediately.
