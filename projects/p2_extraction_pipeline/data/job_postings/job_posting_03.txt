**POSITION AVAILABLE: Senior Aquaculture Technician**

Beacon Blue Fish Farms is seeking an experienced Senior Aquaculture Technician to join our coastal operations team. We are a mid-sized sustainable seafood producer specializing in Atlantic salmon and sea bass farming across three sites in the Pacific Northwest region.

**About the Role**

You'll oversee daily husbandry operations at our primary growing facility, including feeding management, water quality monitoring, health assessments, and equipment maintenance. This is a hands-on position that requires comfort working in wet conditions, often in early mornings or evenings. You'll supervise 2-3 junior technicians and report directly to the Farm Manager. Responsibilities include documenting all animal welfare records, coordinating with our veterinary consultant, and identifying operational inefficiencies that could improve yield or reduce environmental impact.

**What We're Looking For**

- 4+ years in aquaculture, fish farming, or related hatchery environments
- Proficiency with dissolved oxygen meters, pH probes, and basic water chemistry analysis
- Experience recognizing fish disease signs and administering treatments
- Comfort with boat operation and willingness to work on nets in open water
- Ability to lift 50 lbs regularly and work alone during off-shift emergencies
- Valid driver's license

We value problem-solvers who take initiative. Formal certifications aren't required, but relevant training in aquatic animal health or environmental management is preferred.

**Compensation & Schedule**

$48,000–$56,000 annually, plus health insurance and retirement matching. Position is full-time with rotating weekend coverage (approximately twice monthly). Location is near Bandon, OR.

To apply, email resume and three references to careers@beaconblue.net.
