# POSITION: Senior Livestock Management Consultant

## Location
Rural Operations Center, Nebraska (primary), with 40% travel to client farms across Midwest region

## About Us
Heartland Agricultural Solutions has served multi-generational farming families since 1987. We combine traditional animal husbandry knowledge with emerging biotechnology and sustainable practices to optimize herd health and productivity.

## The Role
We're seeking an experienced livestock management consultant to work directly with our portfolio of 200+ beef and dairy operations. You'll conduct on-site assessments, develop customized feeding protocols, troubleshoot animal health issues in collaboration with veterinarians, and implement rotational grazing strategies. This is a hybrid field and office position — expect your weeks to alternate between farm visits and report writing.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Conduct quarterly herd evaluations and prepare detailed management recommendations
- Coordinate with veterinary partners on animal health protocols
- Train farm staff on new management techniques and equipment
- Monitor feed costs and suggest supplier alternatives
- Maintain detailed records in our proprietary management software

## Required Qualifications
- 8+ years working in cattle operations (beef OR dairy)
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
- Ability to work in all weather conditions
- Strong communication skills for non-technical audiences

## Preferred Qualifications
- Certification from American Meat Institute or similar organization
- Experience with grazing management software
- Background in animal science or agricultural business

## Compensation & Benefits
Salary range: $58,000–$72,000 annually, based on experience
- Health, dental, vision insurance
- Fuel stipend: $400/month
- Professional development budget: $1,500/year
- 401(k) matching up to 4%
- 15 PTO days
