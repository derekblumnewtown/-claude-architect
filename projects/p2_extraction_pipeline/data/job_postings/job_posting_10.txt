WAREHOUSE OPERATIONS COORDINATOR
Acme Logistics Solutions

Acme Logistics Solutions is seeking a Warehouse Operations Coordinator to join our rapidly expanding distribution center in the greater Phoenix metropolitan area. This is a unique hybrid role that doesn't fit neatly into traditional warehouse or administrative categories—we need someone who can move fluidly between forklift operations, inventory management systems, and stakeholder communication.

The successful candidate will spend approximately 40% of their time on the warehouse floor managing SKU organization and coordinating with drivers, 35% in our operations office handling database updates and reporting, and 25% participating in continuous improvement initiatives and process documentation. You'll report directly to the Operations Manager and collaborate with cross-functional teams including procurement, quality assurance, and customer service.

Key Responsibilities:
• Monitor real-time inventory levels using our SAP system and reconcile discrepancies
• Operate Class B forklift and pallet jack equipment safely and efficiently
• Create daily pick/pack schedules and troubleshoot workflow bottlenecks
• Conduct monthly cycle counts and maintain documentation for audit purposes
• Train new warehouse staff on safety protocols and standard operating procedures
• Identify process inefficiencies and present recommendations to leadership

Required Qualifications:
Forklift certification (or willingness to obtain within 30 days), proficiency with Microsoft Excel and email systems, ability to lift 50 lbs regularly, valid driver's license, high school diploma or GED. We're looking for someone with logistics or warehouse experience, though we'll consider strong administrative professionals willing to learn warehouse operations.

Preferred: 2-3 years in warehouse/logistics, familiarity with WMS software, bilingual (English/Spanish).

Compensation: $38,000-$42,000 annually, health insurance after 60 days, 401k match at 3%, paid time off.

Location: Phoenix, AZ (no specific zip code provided by employer)
