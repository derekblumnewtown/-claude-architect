## ABOUT THE ROLE

We're seeking an Aquaculture Systems Technician to join our innovative team managing recirculating aquaculture systems (RAS) for sustainable fish farming operations. This is a unique hybrid position combining hands-on technical maintenance with data monitoring and environmental compliance documentation.

Your responsibilities will include:

- Daily monitoring and calibration of water quality parameters (pH, dissolved oxygen, ammonia, nitrite, salinity)
- Maintenance of mechanical and biological filtration systems, including biofilm reactors and settling tanks
- Equipment troubleshooting and preventive maintenance scheduling for pumps, aerators, and control systems
- Collection of water samples and input of environmental data into our custom tracking platform
- Collaboration with the hatchery team on larval rearing protocols
- Weekly reports on system performance and anomalies
- On-call support rotation (1 week per month, including weekends)

## QUALIFICATIONS

**Required:**
- 3-5 years experience in aquaculture, marine biology, or similar technical field
- Strong understanding of RAS principles and fish biology
- Proficiency with water quality testing equipment and basic chemistry
- Mechanical aptitude and comfort working with pumps, filters, and plumbing systems
- Ability to lift 50 lbs regularly and work in wet environments
- Valid driver's license

**Preferred:**
- Experience with tilapia or other warm-water species cultivation
- Familiarity with SCADA systems or automated monitoring platforms
- Certification in aquaculture (IACANZ or similar)
- Experience with environmental permitting processes
