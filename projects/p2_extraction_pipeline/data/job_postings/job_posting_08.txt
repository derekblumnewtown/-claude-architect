# SENIOR FORKLIFT OPERATOR & WAREHOUSE COORDINATOR

**Posting Date:** March 15, 2024

---

Riverside Logistics Solutions is seeking an experienced Senior Forklift Operator & Warehouse Coordinator for our distribution facility in the greater Sacramento area. This is a hybrid operational role that combines hands-on material handling with administrative coordination duties—you'll spend roughly 60% of your time operating equipment and 40% managing inventory systems, coordinating with drivers, and training newer staff members.

**About Us**

We're a family-owned third-party logistics provider specializing in food and beverage distribution. We've been operating for 19 years and pride ourselves on safety, reliability, and treating our team like family.

**What You'll Do**

- Operate forklifts, reach trucks, and order pickers in a fast-paced warehouse environment (OSHA-certified equipment only)
- Coordinate daily shipments and receiving operations; communicate with drivers and route planners
- Maintain accurate inventory records using our WMS system and flag discrepancies
- Train and mentor new warehouse staff on equipment operation and safety protocols
- Conduct pre-shift equipment inspections and report maintenance needs
- Work occasional Saturdays during peak season (roughly 2–4 per month, November–January)

**Requirements**

- Valid forklift certification (or ability to obtain within 30 days of hire)
- Minimum 7 years forklift operation experience in a warehouse or distribution setting
- Familiarity with warehouse management systems (any platform acceptable; training provided for ours)
- Ability to lift 50 lbs regularly, stand for 8-hour shifts
- Clean driving record (you may occasionally drive light trucks for local pickups)
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Reliable transportation to our facility (near Fruitridge, no specific zip code area)

**Preferred Qualifications**

- Reach truck or order picker certification
- Prior experience in food/beverage logistics
- Leadership or mentoring experience
- Bilingual Spanish/English capability

**Compensation & Benefits**

$22.50–$27.00/hour based on experience and certifications. We offer health insurance after 60 days, a 401(k) match (3%), paid time off starting at 15 days annually, and a $500 annual safety bonus if you maintain a clean incident record.

**Schedule**

Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM–2:30 PM (core hours). Peak season may require occasional Saturdays and possible shift adjustments.

**How to Apply**

Submit resume and copies of current certifications to jobs@riversidelogistics.net or apply in person at our facility during office hours (M–F, 8 AM–4 PM). Interviews conducted on rolling basis.
