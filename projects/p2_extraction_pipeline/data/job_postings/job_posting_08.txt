## ABOUT THE ROLE

We are seeking a Senior Environmental Compliance Officer to join our expanding sustainability division at GreenPath Industries. This is a unique opportunity to shape environmental policy implementation across our Pacific Northwest operations while managing regulatory relationships with federal and state agencies.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

- Develop and maintain compliance frameworks for RCRA, CERCLA, and Clean Water Act requirements
- Conduct quarterly environmental audits across 12 regional facilities
- Prepare technical reports for EPA submissions and permit renewals
- Lead cross-functional teams on pollution prevention initiatives
- Serve as primary liaison to Department of Environmental Quality and EPA Region 10
- Train staff on new environmental protocols and maintain documentation systems

## QUALIFICATIONS

**Required:**
- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent professional experience)
- 6+ years in environmental compliance, regulatory affairs, or related role
- Strong knowledge of environmental regulations and permitting processes
- Proficiency with environmental management systems and data analysis
- Excellent written and oral communication skills
- Valid driver's license

**Preferred:**
- Professional certifications (CEMC, PMP, or similar)
- Experience with ISO 14001 implementation
- Familiarity with GIS mapping software
- Knowledge of remediation project management
