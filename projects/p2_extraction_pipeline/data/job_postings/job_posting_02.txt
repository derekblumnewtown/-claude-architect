# Legal Compliance Analyst

**Organization:** Meridian Logistics Solutions

**Location:** Chicago, IL (hybrid - 3 days onsite)

We're seeking a detail-oriented Legal Compliance Analyst to join our Risk & Compliance department. Meridian Logistics Solutions operates across North America with a focus on supply chain optimization and last-mile delivery services.

**About the Role**

You'll be responsible for monitoring regulatory changes across multiple jurisdictions, documenting our compliance posture, and liaising between operations teams and our in-house counsel. This position requires someone who can translate complex regulatory requirements into actionable compliance procedures. You'll maintain our compliance calendar, coordinate internal audits, and prepare quarterly compliance reports for executive leadership.

The ideal candidate has worked in a regulated industry (transportation, logistics, financial services, or healthcare preferred) and understands the intersection of operational constraints and legal requirements. We need someone who asks good questions rather than simply checking boxes.

**Key Responsibilities**

- Track DOT, EPA, and state-level labor regulation changes affecting our operations
- Develop and maintain compliance documentation and standard operating procedures
- Conduct internal compliance audits and remediation follow-ups
- Prepare risk assessment reports identifying potential exposure areas
- Support contract review process to flag compliance considerations

**Required Qualifications**

- 3-5 years experience in compliance, legal operations, or regulatory affairs
- Strong written communication skills (you'll be drafting a lot)
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office and document management systems
- Understanding of logistics or transportation industry a plus, but not required
- Bachelor's degree in business, law, or related field

**What We Offer**

Competitive salary commensurate with experience (we don't disclose ranges publicly, but we're above market for the Midwest), comprehensive health insurance, professional development budget, and a straightforward workplace culture. We close for a week between Christmas and New Year's.
