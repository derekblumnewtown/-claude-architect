# SENIOR MACHINE LEARNING ENGINEER

**Company:** Nexus Data Systems
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid - 3 days onsite)
**Job Type:** Full-Time
**Posted:** November 15, 2024

## About Us

Nexus Data Systems is a mid-stage AI infrastructure company serving Fortune 500 clients in financial services and healthcare. We're building the next generation of model deployment and monitoring platforms.

## The Role

We're seeking a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to lead our model optimization team. You'll own the full lifecycle of ML models in production, from architecture to deployment to observability. This is not a research role—we ship code daily to production systems handling billions of inferences per month.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Design and implement scalable ML pipeline architectures
- Collaborate with data engineers on feature engineering at scale
- Mentor junior ML engineers and conduct technical code reviews
- Identify and resolve model performance degradation in production
- Drive adoption of MLOps best practices across the organization
- Present quarterly technical roadmaps to executive leadership

## What We're Looking For

You should have 6+ years of professional ML engineering experience, with at least 3 years in production ML environments. We need someone who understands the gap between training and serving—you've debugged inference latency issues at scale, dealt with data drift, and shipped models that actually made business impact.

Technical requirements:
- Python (5+ years), experience with Kubernetes
- Deep familiarity with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- SQL and data warehousing concepts
- Comfortable with distributed systems thinking
- Experience with at least one cloud platform (AWS/GCP/Azure)

Nice-to-haves: Rust, experience with feature stores, knowledge of quantization/pruning techniques, background in computer vision or NLP.

We care more about what you've shipped than where you went to school. If you have a portfolio of public ML work (papers, open source, blog posts), please share it.

## Compensation & Benefits

**Equity:** 0.1% - 0.35% options package
**Benefits:** Comprehensive medical/dental/vision, 4 weeks PTO, $10K annual learning budget, home office equipment stipend
