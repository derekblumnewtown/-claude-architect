JUNIOR MARITIME SURVEYOR
Coastal Marine Assessments Ltd. | Maritime Industry | Halifax area

We're seeking a detail-oriented professional to join our growing surveying team. Coastal Marine Assessments Ltd. has been providing comprehensive vessel and port infrastructure evaluations for over two decades, and we're expanding our capacity to meet demand across Atlantic Canada and beyond.

The role involves conducting pre-purchase inspections, damage assessments, and regulatory compliance surveys on commercial and private vessels ranging from fishing boats to mid-size cargo ships. You'll work alongside our senior surveyors, learning industry standards while building your own client base over time. Travel is frequent—expect 40-50% of your time on the water or at various ports. Some overnight stays required.

What we're looking for: Strong mechanical aptitude. Comfort working at heights and in confined spaces. Ability to write clear technical reports. Valid driver's license (class 5 minimum). We don't require prior maritime experience, but relevant background in marine engineering, naval architecture, or ship mechanics is preferred. Familiarity with survey software (Rhino or equivalent CAD tools) is a bonus but not essential—we'll train the right person.

The successful candidate will be comfortable with independent decision-making, possess strong communication skills for client interactions, and demonstrate genuine curiosity about vessel systems. Physical fitness matters here; the job is genuinely demanding.

Compensation and benefits package includes competitive salary (we'll discuss based on experience), extended health coverage after probation, and ongoing professional development support including pathway toward formal surveyor certification.

Send resume, cover letter, and references to: careers@coastalmarine.ca. Applications reviewed on rolling basis. No specific deadline listed.
