# SENIOR MANUFACTURING ENGINEER - ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING

**Company:** Precision Dynamics Corp
**Location:** Salt Lake City, UT (Hybrid - 3 days on-site)
**Job Type:** Full-Time, Permanent
**Posted:** November 14, 2024

---

## Position Overview

Precision Dynamics Corp is seeking a Senior Manufacturing Engineer specializing in additive manufacturing (3D printing) technologies to lead process optimization and quality assurance initiatives across our aerospace and medical device divisions. This is a hands-on technical leadership role reporting directly to the VP of Manufacturing Operations.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Design and implement AM production workflows for complex metal and polymer components
- Conduct failure analysis and root cause investigation on printed parts; develop corrective actions
- Collaborate with design engineering to optimize part geometry for manufacturability via AM processes
- Manage relationships with equipment vendors (SLM, DMLS, FDM systems) and material suppliers
- Train and mentor 2-3 junior manufacturing technicians
- Lead continuous improvement projects targeting cost reduction and cycle time
- Maintain compliance with ISO 13485, AS9100C, and proprietary quality standards
- Participate in new equipment evaluation and qualification

## Required Qualifications

- 8+ years manufacturing engineering experience, minimum 4 years with additive manufacturing technologies
- Proficiency with CAD software (Fusion 360, SolidWorks, or similar)
- Experience with process parameters: laser power, scan speed, layer height, support structure design
- Strong knowledge of post-processing (heat treatment, machining, surface finishing) for AM components
- Familiarity with design of experiments (DOE) and statistical process control (SPC)
- Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, or equivalent professional experience
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred Qualifications

- Certification in additive manufacturing (ASTM, ISO standards)
- Experience with Materialise or similar AM software platforms
- Background in aerospace or medical device manufacturing
- Lean Six Sigma Green Belt or higher
- Patent or publication record in AM technology

## Compensation & Benefits

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage (company pays 85% of premiums)
- 401(k) matching up to 6%
- Annual performance bonus (15-25% of base)

## Work Environment

Monday-Wednesday on-site; Thursday-Friday remote or flexible scheduling. Lab coat and safety footwear required. Exposure to powder-based materials; OSHA safety compliance mandatory. Occasional travel (5-10%) for vendor meetings and training.
