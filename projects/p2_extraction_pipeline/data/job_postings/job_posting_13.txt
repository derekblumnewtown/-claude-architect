MARKETING OPERATIONS COORDINATOR
SyncWave Technologies

About Us
SyncWave Technologies is a mid-stage B2B SaaS company specializing in workflow automation for financial services. We're growing fast and looking for someone to help us scale our marketing infrastructure without losing our scrappy startup culture.

The Role
You'll be the glue holding our marketing tech stack together. We're not looking for someone who just knows Salesforce or HubSpot—we need someone who can think critically about why our processes exist, spot inefficiencies, and actually fix them. Your days will involve setting up marketing automation workflows, managing our CRM database hygiene, coordinating between sales and marketing (they don't always get along), troubleshooting integrations between tools, and occasionally doing some light campaign setup and analytics. This role reports to our VP of Marketing and sits in a weird middle space—part operations, part product, part customer success. We like it that way.

What You'll Need
- Experience with HubSpot, Marketo, or similar platforms (doesn't need to be years, but real hands-on work)
- Comfort with Zapier, Make, or other no-code automation tools
- Ability to write SQL queries or willingness to learn (we use Postgres)
- Strong communication skills—you'll interface with sales, marketing, product, and finance teams regularly
- Obsessive attention to detail, especially with data
- Some exposure to Google Analytics or similar tools

Nice-to-haves: Freshworks, Segment, experience with Salesforce configuration, knowledge of financial services industry, background in customer success operations

Location
Remote (US-based only; we're Eastern and Pacific time zones primarily). We have a small office in Portland, OR if you're ever in the area.

Compensation & Details
$62,000–$74,000 annually, depending on experience. Health/dental/vision insurance, 401k match (3%), professional development budget, unlimited PTO (yes, really—we track it and it averages 18 days), and access to our Slack channel dedicated entirely to GIF sharing.

This is a full-time, permanent role. We're hiring to start ASAP but can wait for the right person. Background check required.

How to Apply
Email your resume and a brief note about a time you fixed something broken in a marketing system (or broke something and had to fix it) to careers@syncwave.io. Please include your timezone. No cover letters needed, but we read everything you send.
