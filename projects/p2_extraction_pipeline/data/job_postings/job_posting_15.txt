POSITION AVAILABLE: Clinical Research Coordinator (PRN/Contract)

Riverside Biomedical Solutions is seeking a detail-oriented Clinical Research Coordinator to support our Phase II and Phase III oncology trials. This is a part-time contract position with flexible scheduling, ideal for someone transitioning back into the workforce or seeking supplementary income.

ABOUT THE ROLE
You'll be the operational backbone of our research operations—managing patient enrollment workflows, ensuring protocol compliance, scheduling study visits, maintaining regulatory documentation, and serving as the primary liaison between our site and the sponsor. Travel to partner clinic locations is required approximately twice monthly (regional, no overnight stays). This role reports directly to the Research Director but works independently much of the time.

REQUIREMENTS
• Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple trials simultaneously
• Experience with electronic data capture (EDC) systems—any platform acceptable
• Reliable transportation (mileage reimbursed)
• Ability to obtain or maintain GCP certification
• High school diploma minimum; some college coursework preferred but not required
• Comfortable learning new software quickly
• Professional demeanor with strong written and verbal communication

NICE TO HAVE
Previous clinical research experience, familiarity with oncology protocols, CPR certification, Spanish language capability.

WHAT WE OFFER
Competitive hourly rate ($19–$26/hour depending on experience), flexible scheduling, professional development support, health insurance eligibility (after 90 days). Equipment and parking provided.

Location: Riverside, CA (no zip provided in original posting)
