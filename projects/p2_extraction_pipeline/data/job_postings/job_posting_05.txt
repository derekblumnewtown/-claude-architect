# Senior Fermentation Scientist

**Company:** Motus Ingredients

**Location:** Madison, WI (hybrid arrangement)

Motus Ingredients is a biotechnology company specializing in precision fermentation for food and beverage applications. We engineer microorganisms to produce sustainable alternatives to animal-derived proteins and specialty ingredients.

**About the Role**

We're seeking a Senior Fermentation Scientist to lead our bioreactor optimization division. You'll oversee scale-up projects from bench-top through pilot production, troubleshoot fermentation kinetics issues, and mentor junior scientists. This role requires someone comfortable with ambiguity—our fermentation processes are novel and occasionally unpredictable, meaning you'll need strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work with incomplete data.

**Key Responsibilities**

- Design and execute fermentation experiments across scales (5L, 50L, 200L bioreactors)
- Monitor dissolved oxygen, pH, temperature, and metabolite production in real-time
- Develop process control strategies and data collection protocols
- Collaborate with upstream strain engineering and downstream purification teams
- Write technical reports and maintain detailed batch records
- Present findings at internal reviews and external conferences as appropriate

**Required Qualifications**

PhD in Microbiology, Bioengineering, or related field; minimum 6 years fermentation industry experience. Proficiency with HPLC, bioreactor software (preferably Sartorius or Applikon), and statistical analysis. Experience with both bacterial and fungal fermentations preferred. Must be comfortable working in a lab environment with biosafety level considerations.

**Compensation & Benefits**

Salary commensurate with experience, estimated $95K–$125K annually. Comprehensive health coverage, 401(k) match, professional development budget ($3K/year), flexible schedule with core hours 9am–3pm.

**Why Join Us**

We're pre-Series B and moving fast. You'll have equity options and direct impact on product commercialization timelines. Leadership has deep fermentation backgrounds and respects scientific rigor.

**To Apply**

Submit resume, cover letter, and one representative publication or technical report to careers@motusingredients.com. Please include your availability and relocation flexibility in your cover letter.
