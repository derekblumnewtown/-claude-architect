# SENIOR MATERIALS SCIENTIST - ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING
**Cedar Ridge Advanced Manufacturing | Cincinnati, OH**

---

We are seeking a Senior Materials Scientist to join our expanding Additive Manufacturing division. Cedar Ridge has pioneered composite 3D printing technology for aerospace and medical device applications over the past 12 years, and we're scaling our R&D team to meet unprecedented demand.

**What You'll Do:**
The ideal candidate will lead material characterization studies, design experimental protocols for novel polymer-ceramic composites, and collaborate with our process engineering team to translate lab discoveries into production specifications. You'll own the full lifecycle from powder synthesis through mechanical testing, thermal analysis, and microstructural evaluation. Some travel to customer sites (approximately 8-12% annually) may be required for application support.

**Your Background:**
- PhD in Materials Science, Chemical Engineering, or Physics (or equivalent industry experience)
- Minimum 6 years post-graduate experience in materials development, preferably in additive manufacturing, composites, or advanced ceramics
- Proficiency with SEM, DSC, TGA, rheology testing, and mechanical testing equipment
- Experience managing lab budgets and cross-functional teams
- MATLAB, Python, or similar computational modeling experience is a plus

We do not require specific CAD experience, though familiarity with 3D modeling software is valued.

**What We Offer:**
Competitive salary range of $125,000–$165,000 depending on experience and background. Comprehensive benefits including health/dental/vision, 401(k) matching (5%), tuition reimbursement, and access to our on-site gym and cafeteria. Flexible work arrangements: 3 days on-site in Cincinnati, 2 days remote.

**About Us:**
Cedar Ridge operates two manufacturing facilities and maintains partnerships with four major university research centers. We are an equal opportunity employer committed to building a diverse workforce.
