**POSITION AVAILABLE**

Artisanal Fermentation Specialist
Wilde & Co. Kombucha Collective
Portland, OR

We are seeking a detail-oriented individual with passion for living cultures and microbial ecology to join our production team. This role sits somewhere between quality control, production management, and research — we're still figuring out the exact boundaries, which means you'll have autonomy to shape it.

Your days will involve monitoring fermentation vessels, maintaining SCOBY health, troubleshooting batch inconsistencies, and documenting everything obsessively. You'll work directly with our head brewer (who is also our founder and has strong opinions about everything) and collaborate with our two-person lab team on flavor development and safety testing.

Requirements: demonstrable experience with fermented foods or beverages (homebrew kombucha counts), comfort with ambiguity, ability to work in a 65-75°F warehouse environment, and genuine curiosity about pH dynamics and bacterial cultures. Formal microbiology background helpful but not required. We need someone who reads labels on probiotic supplements for fun.

This is a full-time hybrid position (3 days on-site minimum). We offer health insurance, 15 days PTO, and kombucha at cost. Salary commensurate with experience; we're transparent that this is a growing company and we can't match tech salaries, but we're committed to fair wages in the 50-65K range depending on what you bring.

Please send résumé, references, and a paragraph about your relationship with fermentation to jobs@wildekombucha.com. No formal application system — we're still small enough to do this humanely.
