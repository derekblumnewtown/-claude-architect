- Demonstrated publication record (15+ peer-reviewed publications preferred)
- Experience managing small research teams
- Strong grant writing skills (NIH or equivalent funding bodies)

Preferred Qualifications
- Previous industry experience in diagnostics or biotech
- Familiarity with machine learning applications in proteomics
- Knowledge of regulatory pathways (FDA 510(k) or similar)
