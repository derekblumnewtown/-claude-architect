# Senior Environmental Compliance Officer

## Company
Riverside Chemical Manufacturing Inc. — A family-owned specialty chemicals producer established in 1987, serving pharmaceutical, agricultural, and industrial markets across North America.

## Location
Baton Rouge, LA (some remote flexibility for documentation and reporting)

## Position Type
Full-time, Exempt

---

## About Us

Riverside Chemical has operated responsibly in the petrochemical corridor for over three decades. We pride ourselves on exceeding regulatory standards and maintaining transparent relationships with our communities and governing bodies. This role is critical to our continued growth and our commitment to environmental stewardship.

## The Role

We seek a Senior Environmental Compliance Officer to lead our environmental management systems, ensure regulatory adherence, and drive continuous improvement initiatives. You'll report directly to our VP of Operations and manage a small team of compliance technicians while interfacing with state and federal agencies, internal departments, and external consultants.

Your responsibilities will include: developing and implementing environmental policies; conducting internal audits and compliance assessments; managing permits and reporting obligations under Clean Air Act, Clean Water Act, RCRA, and Louisiana-specific regulations; responding to inspections; tracking and remediating violations; maintaining environmental documentation; and mentoring junior staff. You'll also champion our ISO 14001 certification renewal and identify cost-effective compliance solutions.

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, Chemistry, or related field
- 7+ years of environmental compliance experience in manufacturing or heavy industry
- Demonstrated knowledge of federal and state environmental regulations
- Experience with environmental management systems and audit processes
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to work independently and lead cross-functional teams

## Preferred Qualifications

- Professional Engineer (PE) license or Environmental Professional (EP) certification
- Experience with air quality permitting or wastewater discharge permits
- Familiarity with environmental databases and monitoring software
- Track record of successful regulatory inspections

## Compensation & Benefits

Salary is commensurate with experience; we anticipate $72,000–$89,000 for well-qualified candidates. We offer comprehensive health insurance, 401(k) matching, professional development support, flexible scheduling, and a collaborative work environment.
