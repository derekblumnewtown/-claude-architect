# Senior Fermentation Scientist - Biotech

**Company:** Meridian Biosciences Inc.

**Location:** Cincinnati, OH (hybrid, 2 days onsite required)

**Job ID:** MB-2024-0847

---

We're seeking a Senior Fermentation Scientist to join our Bioprocess Development team. This is a unique opportunity to work at the intersection of classical microbiology and cutting-edge synthetic biology, supporting our portfolio of diagnostic and therapeutic biologics.

You'll be responsible for designing and optimizing fed-batch and perfusion fermentation processes for mammalian cell lines and microbial systems. Your typical week might involve troubleshooting a scale-up issue in our 500L bioreactor suite, writing SOPs for a new strain, mentoring junior scientists, and presenting findings at our monthly process review. This role reports directly to the Director of Bioprocess Engineering.

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Develop and scale fermentation processes from bench to manufacturing (up to 2000L)
- Conduct design-of-experiments (DoE) for media optimization, temperature, aeration, and agitation parameters
- Collaborate with analytical chemistry and quality assurance on process characterization
- Lead cross-functional projects with commercial operations and regulatory affairs
- Write technical reports and prepare content for regulatory submissions (IND/BLA)
- Train and supervise 1-2 biotech associates

**Required Qualifications:**
- PhD in Bioengineering, Chemical Engineering, Microbiology, or related field (or MS + 5 years industry experience in fermentation/bioprocessing)
- Hands-on experience with mammalian cell culture (CHO, HEK, Vero) or microbial fermentation (E. coli, yeast, insect cells)
- Proficiency with bioreactor control systems and data acquisition software
- Working knowledge of FDA regulations (21 CFR Part 11) and cGMP principles
- Strong statistical analysis and data interpretation skills

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Experience with continuous bioprocessing or ATF/perfusion systems
- Familiarity with single-use bioreactor technology
- Background in vaccine or monoclonal antibody manufacturing
- Experience mentoring junior scientists or leading process development teams

We don't require a specific location zip code, but you should be within reasonable commuting distance of Cincinnati or willing to relocate. Flexible arrangements available for candidates with exceptional expertise.

**Compensation & Benefits:** Competitive salary commensurate with experience (we've budgeted $95K–$140K depending on background). Comprehensive benefits include medical/dental/vision, 401(k) match, tuition reimbursement, and access to our state-of-the-art biomanufacturing facility for professional development.

**Why Join Us:** You'll work on products that matter—our diagnostics reach underserved populations globally, and our pipeline includes biologics addressing serious unmet medical needs. Our bioprocessing team is collaborative, intellectually rigorous, and genuinely interested in advancing the science.

**To Apply:** Submit resume, cover letter, and names of 2 references to bioprocess-careers@meridianbiosciences.com. Applications reviewed on a rolling basis. This posting will remain open until the position is filled.
