## EVENING EXPERIENCE
### $185 per person | $285 with wine pairings

*The menu changes nightly based on market availability, chef's intuition, and a selection of songs chosen by our guests. Each course is served in silence, followed by a brief discussion.*

**COURSE ONE: Arrival**
Fermented black garlic, miso caramel, crispy rice skin, edible flowers (V)

**COURSE TWO: Memory**
A preparation of your childhood comfort food, reimagined

**COURSE THREE: Provocation**
Chef's choice - you will be asked about this one

**COURSE FOUR: Umami**
Bone marrow custard, mushroom leather, anchovy ash, charred bread

**COURSE FIVE: Palate Cleanser**
Sorbet or savory option available upon request

**COURSE SIX: Protein**
Your choice selected during reservation:
- Dry-aged duck breast, turnip, cherry gastrique
- Halibut, brown butter, sea vegetables, finger lime
- Wagyu short rib, fermented black bean, charred scallion
- **VG** Roasted cauliflower steak, miso vinaigrette, toasted seeds

**COURSE SEVEN: Garden**
Seasonal salad with house-made vinaigrette

**COURSE EIGHT: Chaos**
A course involving an element of surprise and guest participation

**COURSE NINE: Sweetness**
Three micro-desserts:
- Dark chocolate, coffee, sea salt
- Yuzu curd, white chocolate, microgreens
- Miso caramel, black sesame, nori crisp

**COURSE TEN: Departure**
Herbal tea, petit four, and a takeaway vinyl record curated by tonight's chef
